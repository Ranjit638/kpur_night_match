Cricket Fun Facts

  • Cricket is one of the oldest team sports in the world, dating back to the 16th century.
  • The longest-ever cricket match lasted for 12 days! It was played between England and South Africa in 1939.
  • Shane Warne, a legendary Australian cricketer, is widely considered one of the greatest bowlers of all time.
  • The fastest-ever ball delivery in cricket was bowled by Shoaib Akhtar at a speed of 161.3 km/h (100.23 mph).

Popular Cricket Formats

  • Test Matches: Played over five days with two innings per side. It's the longest format of the game.
  • ODI (One Day International): A 50-over format where each team bats for 50 overs. Popular in global tournaments like the World Cup.
  • T20: A fast-paced format with 20 overs per side. The IPL is one of the most popular leagues for T20 cricket.
